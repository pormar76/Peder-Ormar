My interests

My interests are strongly connected to sports, media and storytelling. Football has been a big part of my life since I was a kid, and today my goal is to combine that passion with journalism and sports commentary.

Football and my playing career

I started playing football when I was six years old, and from then until I was nineteen I played for Store Bergan IL in Sandefjord. The club became a big part of my everyday life, with training, matches and time spent with teammates on and off the pitch.

In 2021 I was named Player of the Year at Store Bergan IL. That meant a lot to me, because it reflected not only performances on the field, but also effort, attitude and commitment to the team.

I eventually stopped playing for the club when I was 19 due to military service, but football is still a huge part of my life and something I follow closely.

After spending a year at the Norway–Russia border during my military service, I moved back to Sandefjord. There I continued playing football while working at an elementary school to make money and gain experience.

Football has given me discipline, teamwork, and the ability to handle both wins and losses. Those are skills that also transfer well into journalism and media.

From Sandefjord to the US

After working for a while, I decided it was time to study. Since I have always wanted to work with sports commentary, I chose a bachelor in journalism and started studying in Norway.

Later, I got the chance to study in the United States. That is how I ended up here at UWRF, working on projects like this website, the Gift & Thrift event and different journalism assignments.

Studying abroad has given me a new perspective on media, language and culture, and it has confirmed that I want to continue working with sports and storytelling in some form.

Sports commentary and media

One of my biggest interests is sports commentary. When I watch football or other sports, I do not only follow the game – I also listen closely to how the commentators work. I pay attention to their energy, language, timing and how they build excitement.

I like analyzing matches, following different leagues and sometimes doing my own “test commentary” while watching games. This helps me understand what works well, and it brings me a bit closer to my long-term goal of working in sports media.

Experiences like helping to cover the Ski World Championships and working with events such as UWRF Gift & Thrift have also given me insight into how communication and media work behind the scenes.

Gaming, podcasts and music

Gaming

In my free time I enjoy gaming, especially EAFC and Star Wars Battlefront II. I also sometimes play Fortnite with friends. Gaming is a way for me to relax, compete and stay connected with people back home.

Podcasts

I often listen to sports podcasts such as B-Laget and Spårtsklubben. They are funny, entertaining and a great way to stay updated on what happens in the world of sports, while also getting inspiration from how people talk about games and players.

Music

When it comes to music, I mostly listen to pop, rap and Norwegian music. It is what I use when I travel, train, game or just need to clear my head. Music is a good way to change or boost my mood.

Other sports I follow and enjoy

Golf

I also enjoy playing golf whenever I get the chance. It’s a sport that requires focus, patience and a calm mindset, which makes it very different from football. I like the challenge of improving small parts of my game and the social aspect of playing with friends.

Formula 1

I follow Formula 1 as well. Even though I don’t support one specific team, I enjoy watching Red Bull and Max Verstappen. What I like most about F1 is the mix of strategy, speed and the personalities both on and off the track. It’s a sport where every second matters, and that makes it exciting to follow.

Skiing

Growing up in Norway, skiing has always been a part of life. I’m not a professional by any means, but I love going out on the slopes or doing a cross-country session when I can. Skiing is one of the best ways to enjoy winter, stay active, and spend time with friends.
