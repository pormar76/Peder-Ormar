My favorites

This page is all about my top 3 choices in different areas. If you want to get a quick picture of what I like the most, this is the place.

Top 3 songs

  1. "Superbowl (Ja eller nei)" – Mario S
  2. "Working" – Roc Boyz
  3. "Highway to Hell" – AC/DC

These songs represent the kind of energy and style I like: a mix of Norwegian music, rap and classic rock.

Top 3 movies

  1. Star Wars: Episode V – The Empire Strikes Back
  2. Gladiator
  3. Inglourious Basterds

I like movies with strong stories, memorable characters and big moments. Star Wars will always be special, but films like Gladiator and Inglourious Basterds are right up there as well.

Top 3 football players

  1. Fernando Torres
  2. Virgil van Dijk
  3. Erling Braut Haaland

These players all mean something different to me. Torres is a big favorite from earlier years, Van Dijk represents modern Liverpool and leadership, and Haaland is one of the most exciting Norwegian players ever.
